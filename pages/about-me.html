Hi there! My name is Ric. Welcome to my learning journal.

I am a Scrimba student currently enrolled in the Frontend Developer Career Path Pro Plan. I am a retired Information Systems developer. I have started this exciting new journey to renew my skills and become a skilled frontend developer.

Focus On Learning

You are never too young or old to learn. I have never used a learning journal before now. However, now that I have started using this one, I find that maintaining a journal definitely has its merits. Reviewing the work and any notes I made during the day and then writing my thoughts down, clarifies and deepens my understanding of the concepts and techniques I learned that day.

Comprehension and Retention

Scrimba likes to call it "develop your muscle memory" by avoiding using copy/paste when you write your code. I have found that this learning technique promotes the retention of what I have learned in the module I'm working on. Understanding plus retention creates confidence for me in design, coding and deployment.
